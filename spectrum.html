Political Spectrum

Information Design Class

About

As a group of three, we built an online website that helped people to understand Canadian politics and make better political decisions.

Main Take Away

Building a website while considering 4 layers of design considerations- interface design, navigational design, information design, and user experience design.

Challenge

We were asked to build an online tool that can help 3 personas which represent different demographics, to make political decisions. Our group decided to build a website which helps people to choose a political party that best suits their needs.

Process

We started with analyzing our 3 different personas, defining criteria as to what they may be seeking from a political website. After defining their different behavior patterns and needs, we came up with 20 questions and generated a flow chart to illustrate a hierarchy of the information and to see where each answers would direct the personas.

We then generated a series of wireframes using Axure that illustrates how the person may step through the decision assistant. We tested our wireframes with our classmates in order to see how users might perceive our design decisions.

Result

Our website successfully helped our personas to understand Canadian politics and make better political decisions using 4 layers of design considerations,

    • Interface Design
    • The big and contrasting buttons allow users to quickly recognize their activity in a simple and profound way.
    • Navigational Design
    • Above the questions, the website shows which section of the questionnaire the user is currently at. On the bottom the user can navigate to previous or next pages.
    • Information Design
    • Based on the persona analysis, we came up with the 5 most popular political issues and grouped questions in an individual political issue. Throughout the questionnaire, the sub-header clearly explains about the political issue for users.
    • Emotional Design
    • Our website uses a patterned background in order to make it visually pleasing to users, because design influences emotional engagement.

Take Away

  1. I practiced working with personas and analyzing the data to help personas make political decisions.
  2. I practiced building a website using HTML/CSS while considering 4 layers of design considerations- interface design, navigational design, information design, and user experience design.
